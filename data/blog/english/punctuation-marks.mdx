本文总结了常用英文标点符号与数学符号对应的读法和中文含义，并对它们做了一个基础的分类。

This article will introduce and classify the basic punctuation marks and mathematic signs.

# Basic Punctuation Marks

## Brackets

| Marks | Prounancation               | Chinese          |
| ----- | --------------------------- | ---------------- |
| ()    | parenthesis, round brackets | 圆括号           |
| []    | square brackets             | 方括号           |
| \<>   | Angle brackets              | 尖括号           |
| \{}   | curly brackets, braces      | 大括号           |
| 《》  | French quotes               | 法文引号；书名号 |

# Mathematic Signs

## Operators

| Signs | Prounancation        | Chinese    |
| ----- | -------------------- | ---------- |
| ＋    | plus                 | 加号；正号 |
| －    | minus                | 减号；负号 |
| ±     | plus or minus        | 正负号     |
| ×     | is multiplied by     | 乘号       |
| ÷     | is divided by        | 除号       |
| √     | (square) root        | 平方根     |
| ∪     | union of             | 并，合集   |
| ∩     | intersection of      | 交，通集   |
| ∫     | the integral of      | …的积分    |
| ∑     | (sigma) summation of | 总和       |

| ≌     | is equal to or approximately equal to | 等于或约等于号 |
| ≈     | is approximately equal to             | 约等于号       |
